Registration

  1. Just click Start for free and after the first lesson, you will be asked to register. You can also click the Register button at the top of the screen to register at any time.
  2. You will then be asked for your personal information (First Name, Last Name, Email, and Password), this will be used to create an account. Write down the email address and password you use, so that you can access your account in the future.
  3. If you are taking the course for insurance purposes only, mark the corresponding checkbox. Then click Continue.
  4. If you are taking the course to get a ticket dismissed, select the court that issued the ticket. You also have the option to enter the date the certificate is due to the court. Then click Continue.
  5. Select one of the packages of products on the left side of the screen or select the Custom Package to choose exactly which products you want. Select the shipping choice for your certificate. Enter your billing information and click Pay Now.
  6. After you have paid, the course will begin. If you have started work on the course prior to paying, the course will resume where it left off.
